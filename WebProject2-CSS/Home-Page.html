AEW is a wrestling orginazation created by pro wrestler Cody Rhodes. It started with the show Dynamite on Wendesday nights on the network TNT. When it started the popularity grew even during the challenging times of the pandemic. Since then it had many shows and pay-per-veiws. Recently, they have came up with the one hour show Rampage on Friday nights.
Many wrestlers from many promotions can express their creative freedom and embrace their characters. Many wrestlers from various promotions, even WWE, quickly joined AEW to explore these benefits and many other things the company has to offer. The company is run by Tony Khan, owner of the Jacksonville Jaguars.
